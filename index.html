About Fitness Tracker

Nutrition means getting the food and nourishment that you need for health and growth.

According to the World Health Organization, a nutrient is a substance required by the body for survival, growth, and reproduction. In other words, nutrients are what give us energy and allow our bodies to perform their essential functions. Every organism on our earth needs nutrients; they are necessary for life!

The Meal tracker will help you to give your preferred Healthy Meal of the day.

A well-balanced diet provides all of the:

energy you need to keep active throughout the day nutrients you need for growth and repair, helping you to stay strong and healthy and help to prevent diet-related illness, such as some cancers Keeping active and eating a healthy balanced diet can also help you to maintain a healthy weight.

There are two types of essential nutrients: macronutrients and micronutrients. Macronutrients include protein, carbohydrates, fats, and water. Micronutrients include vitamins and minerals.

We help you to have a balanced diet.

Regular exercise helps prevent or manage many health problems and concerns, including:

Stroke, Metabolic syndrome, High blood pressure, Type 2 diabetes, Depression, Anxiety, Many types of cancer Arthritis Falls It can also help improve cognitive function and helps lower the risk of death from all causes.

Exercise delivers oxygen and nutrients to your tissues and helps your cardiovascular system work more efficiently.

This fitness tracker will help you to track your physical fitness daily

Physical activity can help you think, learn, problem-solve, and enjoy an emotional balance. It can improve memory and reduce anxiety or depression. Regular physical activity can also reduce your risk of cognitive decline, including dementia
